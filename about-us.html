About Us

Ceylon Development Funding Force Pvt Ltd (CDFF) is a value-driven financial institution committed to supporting Sri Lanka’s economic development through inclusive and responsible financial services.We believe that financial access is a catalyst for sustainable national progress.

By empowering individuals, entrepreneurs and underserved communities with structured financial solutions, CDFF contributes directly to economic mobility, social equity and long-term financial resilience.

Our operations are built on trust, transparency, disciplined governance and a fulfilled workforce that drives responsible growth.

Mission & Vision

Our Vision: Supporting Sri Lanka’s economic development through a fulfilled and committed workforce.

Our Mission: To provide sustainable financial solutions, cultivate a committed workforce, and build trusted relationships that drive the growth of all stakeholders.

Our goals are anchored around four key pillars that guide our strategy, operations, and long-term vision:

Financial Stability: Achieving sustainable financial strength through disciplined, responsible, and ethical lending practices.

Employee Engagement & Well-being: Fostering a supportive, inclusive, and performance-driven workplace that enhances employee satisfaction, development, and retention.

Social Equity & Inclusion: Promoting fairness, equal opportunity, and responsible financial access to enable broader participation in economic growth.

Sustainable Business Expansion: Driving continuous innovation, operational excellence, and strategic growth to expand our portfolio of solutions and market presence.

Customer Base & Market Reach

CDFF proudly serves a growing and diverse customer base across Sri Lanka, reflecting the trust placed in our financial solutions and service standards.

Our current portfolio includes:

  • 10,000+ customers under Small Business Administration (SBA) Loans
  • 600+ customers utilizing Pawning facilities
  • 280+ customers under Motor Loan financing

These figures demonstrate our strong presence within the micro and small enterprise segment, as well as our expanding footprint in asset-backed and mobility financing solutions.
