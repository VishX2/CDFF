Our Loan & Financial Services

CDFF offers practical and customer-focused financing solutions designed to support personal, business, and short-term financial needs through transparent and structured lending.

Micro Loans

CDFF Micro Loans are designed to support small-scale business ventures and personal financial needs with flexible repayment terms.

Motor Loans

CDFF Motor Loans offer affordable and structured vehicle financing solutions for personal and commercial use.

Mortgage Loans

Our Mortgage Loan solutions provide customers with access to long-term financing for property purchase, construction, or improvement.

Business Loans

CDFF Business Loans are structured to strengthen small and medium-scale enterprises by providing capital for expansion, equipment purchase, operational enhancement, and cash flow management.
